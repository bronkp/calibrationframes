Calibration Frames

Calibration frames allow astrophotographers to correct for noise in their images

Light Frames

The most obvious method for correction is to take multiple photos and average out their data.

Flat Frames

While averaging can help correct noise, some errors, like vignetting, will be consistent across samples. To correct for this, a capture is taken of what should be a consistently illuminated image. Doing this we can find what errors the system contains and correct for them.
